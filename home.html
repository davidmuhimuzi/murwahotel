In need of a place to escape stress?

Escape to paradise at our charming and eco-friendly bed & breakfast nestled in the breathtaking surroundings of Lake Kivu. Experience the perfect blend of comfort, serenity, and sustainability in the heart of nature.

At Maravilla Kivu Eco Resort, expect a warm welcome and exceptional hospitality throughout your stay. Indulge in delicious meals, including small Sambaza fish freshly caught from Lake Kivu and exotic fruits handpicked from the resort's fantastic garden. Breathe in the fresh air and take delight in observing over 24 species of birds that call the garden home. Relax and meditate in this serene haven, offering a restful atmosphere for rejuvenation.
